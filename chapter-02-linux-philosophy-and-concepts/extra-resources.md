- Linux now runs on Android phones, meaning billions of people use 
  Linux daily without knowing it
- Technically the full name is GNU/Linux because the Linux kernel is 
  combined with GNU tools to make a complete OS — but most people 
  just say Linux
- There are hundreds of Linux distros in existence but most of them 
  are based on one of the three main families covered in the course
