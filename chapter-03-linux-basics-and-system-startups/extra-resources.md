# Chapter 03: Extra Resources
> These are things I looked up outside of the course to understand 
> the chapter better. They are not part of the LFS101 content.

---

- BIOS can only handle hard drives up to 2TB. UEFI supports much 
  larger drives and also has a Secure Boot feature that blocks 
  unauthorized software from loading at startup
- The commands to explore the filesystem like `ls /`, `df -h`, 
  and `dmesg` are not taught until Chapter 7 of the course
- systemd starts services in parallel which is why modern Linux 
  boots faster than older systems that used SysVinit
